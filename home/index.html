About

The “E-DONATION.COM” is a Web based donation portal which will be mainly used for helping the patient,farmers and NGO’s who needs donation for fulfillment of their needs.The specification builds on the experience of the users of IT in money donation and interconnecting all the Patients, Farmers and NGO’S into a single network with donors. The Centralised database of the application would be hosted on World Wide Web. The app would cover the scope of all the needy Patients, Farmers, NGO’s and other people worldwide who are willing to donate.
